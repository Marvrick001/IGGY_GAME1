Welcome to Iggy

🦎 Adopt your pet, mine for free, play games, and build your fortune! Earn passive income while offline and watch your multipliers grow!

How to Play

1 Adopt Iggy: Pay 50K to adopt your pet (required for everything!)
2 Feed & Hydrate: Keep hunger/thirst above 0 to play games
3 Start Mining: Earn 1 point/sec FREE (works even when offline!)
4 Play Games: Crash (predict when rocket crashes) or Flip (heads/tails)
5 Upgrade Pet: Every 5 levels = +0.5x multiplier (Levels 1-4: 1.0x, 5-9: 1.5x, etc)
6 Mining: Earns based on pet level (1-4: 1 pts/sec, 5-10: 10 pts/sec) - Works offline!

Game Modes

🚀
Crash

Watch the rocket climb with a glowing aura! Cash out before it crashes. Winnings = Bet × Multiplier × Pet Multiplier!

🪙
Flip

Flip a coin - heads doubles your bet, tails you lose it. Same multiplier system as Crash!

⛏️
Mining

FREE passive income! Earn 1 point every second. Works even when you're offline!

Rules & Tips

• MUST ADOPT: Pay 50K first or you can't do anything!
• Hunger & thirst decrease over time (feed/hydrate to keep playing)
• Each game costs 10 hunger + 15 thirst
• Mining is FREE and the best way to start earning (no cost!)
• Multiplier = (Game Multiplier) × (Pet Level Multiplier)
• Pet Wealth tracks your total investments & earnings
• Mining earns more at higher pet levels
• Rocket glows brighter as multiplier increases! 🟢
